Welcome! I finally made a home for my pottery — a place to share finished pieces
and to keep an honest record of how they came to be.

I'll use this journal to write about the things that don't show up in a photo: the
glaze that came out completely different than I expected, the mug handle I re-pulled
four times, the satisfying *ping* of a well-fired bowl cooling in the kiln.

If you're here for the pots, head over to the [gallery](/). If you're here for the
process, stick around — I'll be posting as I go.
